How to get over fear of failure

As someone once said - The first lesson of any new venture - "Disappointment". Fear of failure is something that is natural to human beings and it has its evolutionary basis. Humans are the only species that can remember their past and plan for the future. Planning for the future and sometimes being scared for the future helped our ancestors to move on to the top of the food chain. Our mind is constantly trying to figure out where we stand in the present and to do that it gathers information and memories from the past both good and bad and projects a future based on the past data. It is normal for humans to be scared about the future and also to remember our past so that we can learn from our mistakes and not repeat them in the future. The by-product of this process may sometimes result in anxiety and panic which is completely normal. Our Mind wants to do easy and predictable things, it wants security and not growth and growth never happens in the comfort zone.
